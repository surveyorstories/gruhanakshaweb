GRUHANAKSHA

A powerful QGIS plugin to support the SVAMITVA scheme. Streamline cadastral mapping and generate standardized outputs with efficiency and precision.

Everything You Need for SVAMITVA Success

Gruhanaksha is packed with features designed to make your cadastral mapping tasks faster, easier, and more accurate.

Automated Workflows

Accelerate your mapping process with tools designed for SVAMITVA deliverables, reducing manual effort.

Standardized Outputs

Generate maps, reports, and data in formats that comply with the official SVAMITVA scheme guidelines.

Data Validation

Ensure data integrity with built-in validation checks and quality control tools tailored for cadastral data.

Intuitive Interface

Designed for GIS professionals, the user-friendly interface integrates seamlessly into the QGIS environment.

Batch Processing

Efficiently process large datasets and multiple village areas at once, saving valuable time and resources.

Dynamic Symbology

Apply rule-based styling and labeling automatically to visualize your cadastral data clearly and consistently.
